About

More About Me.

I am a second-year mathematics student at The University of Nottingham. My current mathematical interests are in applied mathematics, especially dynamical systems, computational mathematics.

I am a problem solver, especially love solving interesting problems by mathematical modelling and computational simulations, like optimizing sales for e-commerce, image analysis for water quality, population growth forcast, simulation for ballistics,similarity analysis for languages, network model for musicians...

I am a student member of SIAM and a regular participant in SIAM events. I used to be a drum player in orchestra and a guitar player in a rock band. When I am not doing mathematics, I always play the guitar. I am also a language lover and I can speak a little bit Russian and Cantonese. Just like mathematics, languages also use smybols to decipher the world. I love reading books, both math and non-math: especially books about history and culture.
