About

More About Me.

I am a third-year mathematics student at The University of Nottingham. My current mathematical interests are in applied mathematics, especially dynamical systems, computational mathematics.

I am a problem solver, especially love solving interesting problems by mathematical modelling and computational simulations, like optimizing sales for e-commerce, image analysis for water quality, population growth forcast, simulation for ballistics,similarity analysis for languages, network model for musicians...

I am a student member of SIAM and IMA, and a regular participant in events of these two societies. I love music and I used to be a drum player in School's orchestra and a guitar player in a rock band. Now when I am not doing mathematics, I always play the guitar. I am also a language lover and in my spare time, I often learn German, Russian and Cantonese. I love reading books and travelling, which are good ways to learn and experience local history and culture.

Work and Education

Experience

Summer 2020

School of Geographical Science, University of Nottingham Ningbo China

Reserach Assistance

I did a research about the Austronesian language family supervised by Dr. Tengwen Long. Hypothesis shows that the Austronesian originated from Southern China, and I use quantatitive mehtod based on MATLAB and python to study the language distance in order to verify this kind of hypothesis. Language clusters were found and phylogenetic tree was also built. Same method also works for Indo-European language family. The code and report are accessible on Github.

Summer 2020

Ningbo Jieyou Information Tech. Co. Ltd, Ningbo China

Front-end Developer

I was responsible for front-end development for a WeChat mini programme named AllLink which is a campus forum that allows verified university students and staffs to share information and send anonymous posts and comments. I realised interactive features of web pages (e.g. subscription, notification and like) and collaborated with back-end developers to create an API and I designed a search engine for the mini programme by utilizing WeChat cloud development platform. This app had over 1,000 daily active users with an average using time ranging from 15 to 20 minutes; Service had extended to around 10 universities in China.

Summer 2021

School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Nottingham, UK

Summer Internship, funded by University of Nottingham

I studied the dynamical system for 2d Electron gases supervised by Dr. Martin Richter and establish a model based on transfer operator method. Such model, which was realised by MATLAB, could account for some chaotic behaviour mearsured by experiment for 2DEG in a certain geometirc cavity from a computational simpler view. Bifurcation was also found by Poincaré section. The code and poster are accessible on Github.

Summer 2021

School of Geographical Science, University of Nottingham Ningbo China

Research Assistance

This research is supervised by Dr. Meili Feng. Based on Google Earth Engine, in order to obtain a more intuitive water resources distribution map of Ningbo, four methods: WNDWI, NDWI, MNDWI and AWEI were used to analyze and process water information, and computer experimental methods such as stratified sampling and matrix calculation were used to conduct experimental error analysis, which is a big methodological improvement compared with the traditional GIS.
